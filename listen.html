blvck

Nov 22nd, 2015 was the day he knew he would be here forever. That day his 1st song was recorded and he never looked back!

BLVCK, a native of Raleigh creates music that causes you to get lost in the deep cuts and vibrant lyrics and feel good about yourself all at the same time. His music is a vibe that can not be duplicated because it's so hard to find a singer/rapper with that much talent.

From live shows as a kid, to headlining local artist shows he has done it. He puts on a hell of a show! Most young artists can't do that or have the charisma to make it far. Young Drake, Big Sean, and PND come to mind when we hear his music but with his own twist!
